Coach notes

Old-school bodybuilding discipline, modern durability language.

The Emrick-style lens belongs here: feel the target muscle, control the negative, respect the joint, and teach the student to own the rep.

STEP 01

Assess

Observe squat, hinge, push, pull, plank, and shoulder motion. Start where the client actually is.

STEP 02

Anchor

Choose one main lift or movement pattern. Give it the freshest attention and best coaching.

STEP 03

Balance

Pair pressing with pulling, squatting with hinging, and heavy work with mobility or stability.

STEP 04

Progress

Add load, range, reps, or density only when form stays clean and recovery remains good.
